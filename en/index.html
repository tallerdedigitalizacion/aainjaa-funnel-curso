Frequently asked questions

Short, clear answers that can be polished later with final commercial details.

When do I get access?

After payment is validated, the backend can approve access and send instructions or unlock the private area.

Which language is the product in?

The site works in Spanish and English. Product language can be defined by pack or by the backend response.

Do I need prior experience?

Not necessarily, although the current recommended level is intermediate. This copy remains editable by locale.

Can I pay with Stripe?

Yes. The form is prepared to redirect to checkout or a payment link when the backend or config says so.

Is manual payment available?

Yes. The backend response can return manual payment instructions plus a reference code.

Does the product include physical material?

Only if the selected pack requires it. In that case shipping fields appear and availability messaging can be shown.

How long does delivery take?

Digital access is usually fast after validation. If physical material is included, timelines are indicative and may vary due to logistics or customs.

What is private access?

It is the placeholder entry point for the future password-protected area for students or buyers.
